## Motivation for this Blog

Why everyone should micro-blog or at least leave a place on the internet that belongs to just you: the idea that having digital independence and having a place completely yours to share ideas, thoughts, and things you are working on or interested in without any filtering or flagging is amazing. Back in the day, people used to share thoughts on these tiny websites made simply in Markdown or HTML. Those sites often looked rough, but they were a direct piece of someone's brain—like a mini book that someone decided to put online. It's a cool way to learn about someone in a fun, honest way. You can build something yourself and publish it on GitHub, or, if you prefer hosted options, you can use services like [Bear Blog](http://bearblog.dev/) or [Micro.blog](https://micro.blog/) or something similar.
